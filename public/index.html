Getting started

The Dewey Design System aims to provide a set of reusable components that can be used and adapted to build a wide variety of website and web applications. The intention is that these different elements work together well and can be interchanged with a degree of modularity.

It provides a flexible and esy to use starting point to develop your website, but may need additional features or adaptions specific to your project.

Benefits

  • Consistency across different products, creating a sense of familiarity for our users (both students and educators)
  • Rapid prototyping at increased fidelity
  • Quicker progression from design to development
  • Easier to make changes across a number of products at once

Usage

Dewey extends the bootstrap framework. View detailed documentation about this framework.
